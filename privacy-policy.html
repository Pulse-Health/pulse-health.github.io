4. Apple HealthKit

Data Collected: Heart rate, workout data, fitness metrics, sleep data from Apple Watch and iPhone.

Storage: This data remains on your device and your private iCloud. Pulse only reads it with your explicit permission.

Important: HealthKit data cannot be shared with third parties without your permission. We never sell this data.

5. Supabase Data Storage

Stores: User profile, nutrition logs, workout history

Location: 🇪🇺 European Union (EU servers)

Protection: Your data is fully protected by GDPR. Supabase servers are hosted in the EU, so there are no data transfer concerns.

Data Processing Agreement: Yes, Supabase is DPA-compliant

Retention: Your data is stored on Supabase until you delete your account. When you delete your account, all data is permanently deleted.

6. Clerk Authentication

Provider: Clerk, Inc.

Stores: Your email address and authentication/session data used to create your account, sign in, and keep you logged in

Location: 🇺🇸 United States

Purpose & Legal Basis: Account creation, login, and session management (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR — performance of the contract to provide the app).

International Transfer: Because Clerk processes data in the United States, your account data is transferred to a third country. This transfer is safeguarded by the EU Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) agreed under our Data Processing Agreement with Clerk.

Data Processing Agreement: Yes, in place with Standard Contractual Clauses.

7. OpenAI API (AI Coaching)

Legal basis: Two separate mandatory consents are required during onboarding before AI Coaching is activated: (1) explicit consent to the processing of your health data for AI coaching purposes (Art. 9(2)(a) GDPR); and (2) explicit consent to the transfer of that data to OpenAI in the United States (Art. 49(1)(a) GDPR). Both consents are mandatory — AI Coaching is not available unless both are given. You may withdraw either consent at any time by disabling AI Coaching in app settings; this does not affect the lawfulness of processing carried out before withdrawal.

Data Sent to OpenAI per coaching request:

  • Your preferred name (display name from your profile)
  • Your exact age
  • Your biological sex (from Apple Health or manual entry)
  • Your height and weight (from Apple Health or manual entry)
  • Your coaching question (the message you typed)
  • Relevant memory snippets — up to 5,000 characters of saved coaching context and notes stored in the app
  • Health analysis summaries — computed scores and trend analyses produced on your device (not raw sensor readings such as individual HRV samples)

What is NOT sent to OpenAI: Your email address, Clerk authentication data, raw HealthKit sensor readings (e.g. individual heart-rate measurements), or your complete chat history.

Location: 🇺🇸 United States (OpenAI servers)

Retention: OpenAI retains API request data for up to 30 days by default. We have requested a shorter retention period from OpenAI; this page will be updated once a shorter period is confirmed.

Protection: OpenAI uses Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for EU data transfers to ensure GDPR compliance.

Data Processing Agreement: Yes, available at: https://openai.com/de-DE/policies/row-privacy-policy/

Opt-out: You can disable AI coaching in the app settings.

8. Food Database Lookups (Nutrition Feature)

When you search for foods or scan a barcode in the nutrition feature, Pulse sends the search term or barcode number to one or both of the following public food databases to retrieve nutritional information. No health data, account data, or personal identifiers are included in these requests — only the food name or barcode number and your device's IP address (inherent to any internet request).

Service Operator Location Data sent Legal basis
USDA FoodData Central U.S. Department of Agriculture 🇺🇸 United States Food search term or barcode number; device IP address Performance of contract / Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(b)/(f) GDPR). Transfer to a third country on the basis of Art. 49(1)(c) GDPR (public interest / official government database).
OpenFoodFacts Open Food Facts (French non-profit) 🇪🇺 European Union Food search term or barcode number; device IP address Performance of contract / Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(b)/(f) GDPR). No third-country transfer.

Results are cached locally on your device; search terms are never stored on Pulse's servers. Photos taken during barcode scanning are processed entirely on-device and are never transmitted anywhere.
